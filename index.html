Circuit Features

A FIA-standard and JAF-certified racing circuit designed with environmental consciousness, featuring a 30-meter forest buffer zone and 10,000 newly planted trees. The facility accommodates various motorsport activities including:

  • Sports driving sessions
  • Two and four-wheel racing events
  • Cycling competitions
  • Vehicle and tire testing
  • Photography sessions
